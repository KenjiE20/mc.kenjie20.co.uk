* Version: 1.21.5
* Can be joined with a Vanilla client, but having some client-side extra mods is _highly_ recommended.
  * [Lithium](https://modrinth.com/mod/gvQqBUqZ)
  * [Sodium](https://modrinth.com/mod/AANobbMI)
  * [Bobby](https://modrinth.com/mod/M08ruV16)
  * [Iris](https://modrinth.com/mod/YL57xq9U)
  * [BetterF3](https://modrinth.com/mod/8shC1gFX)
  * [Mod Menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/mOgUt4GM)
  * [Simple Voice Chat](https://modrinth.com/mod/9eGKb6K1)
  * [AudioPlayer](https://modrinth.com/mod/SRlzjEBS)
  * [No Chat Reports](https://modrinth.com/mod/qQyHxfxd)
  * [Peek](https://modrinth.com/mod/TnOXNf5e)
* Has a variety of [Vanilla Tweaks](https://vanillatweaks.net) datapacks to further enhance things;
  * Descriptions, info and commands visible in the advancements window (`L`)
  * Decorative-cosmetic
    * silence mobs
    * player head drops
    * more mob heads
    * mini blocks
  * Convenience
    * spectator night vision
    * spectator conduit power
    * multiplayer sleep[^1]
    * fast leaf decay
  * Gameplay-changes
    * anti enderman grief
  * Informative
    * track statistics
    * track raw statistics
    * durability ping[^1]
    * afk display

[^1]: Has config elements
